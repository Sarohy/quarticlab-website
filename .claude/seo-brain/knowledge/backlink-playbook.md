# Backlink & Digital-PR Playbook (operational summary)

Full evidence: `research-dossier.md` §5. This is the ranked, week-to-week version `p6-backlinks` consumes.

**Prerequisite (do not skip):** Phase 1 discoverability fixes (blog→sitemap, `Seo.jsx`, internal links) must ship first. Links/citations earned before pages are crawlable + equity-passing are wasted.

**Anchor governance:** ~35–50% branded, 10–15% naked URL, 15–25% generic, ≤15% exact-match. Never commercial exact-match anchors from paid/guest placements (named in Google's Dec 2025 spam policy).

## Ranked by ROI (work top-down)

1. **Directory tier — Clutch first, then claim the rest.** Clutch, GoodFirms, DesignRush, TheManifest, G2, Crunchbase, LinkedIn Company Page. Complete service taxonomy matching `services`/`service_details`; phone-verified reviews. *Why #1:* doubles as an AI-citation source AND a bottom-funnel lead source; verified Clutch reviews auto-feed TheManifest "best-of" lists. **NAP byte-identical** to `_app.js` PostalAddress.
2. **Quarterly original-research digital PR.** Mine real Quartic Lab delivery data → one data campaign → pitch via Featured.com (rehomed HARO) + Qwoted + direct journalist outreach. Earns DR50-85 editorial links + durable AI citations. Pair with the `add-blog` original-data posts.
3. **"Best AI/agentic/Next.js dev companies 2026" listicle inclusion.** Use an AI-visibility tool to find which domains are cited for your target prompts, then pursue those exact lists (reputational ingestion lag ~30–90 days).
4. **Founder/lead-engineer thought leadership.** LinkedIn personal posts AND the Company Page (ChatGPT/Google pull member posts; Perplexity pulls Company Pages). Expert sourcing 3–5×/week via Featured/Qwoted using a *named human*. Builds the personal entity LLMs associate with the brand.
5. **Ecosystem/partner links.** Cloud/DevOps/AI-tooling partner pages, alliance member pages → link to the matching `service_details`/case study. Safest dofollow links for an agency.
6. **Enrich `sameAs` + convert unlinked mentions.** Add Clutch/Crunchbase/GitHub to `_app.js` Organization `sameAs`; find unlinked brand mentions and request links.
7. **Genuine non-promotional Reddit/YouTube presence** for the platforms AI cites (directional) — niche dev/startup/AI subreddits, transcript-rich explainer videos repurposed from research posts.

## AVOID (never generate these tasks)

PBNs, bulk paid dofollow links, mass low-quality directory submissions, scaled generalist guest posts, niche edits on thin aged domains. All devalued to zero equity since March 2026 and profile-polluting. Run ONE backlink audit; disavow only clearly toxic patterns.

## Tracker

`p6-backlinks` maintains `outputs/backlinks-tracker.md`: a table of every directory/profile (claimed? complete? NAP-correct? review count) + a digital-PR pipeline (pitch → outlet → status) + `sameAs` checklist. The brain checks it each run and emits the next 1–2 highest-ROI un-done items as `backlink`/`local` tasks.
